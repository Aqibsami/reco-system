Turning Waste into Value

Recycle Economy System

RECO is a revolutionary platform that transforms everyday waste into a valuable digital asset. We are building a complete circular economy that benefits communities, industries, and the planet.

See How It Works

The RECO Circular Economy

1

Waste Collection

Local authorities and partners collect and oversee segregated waste, forming the foundation of the system.

2

Recycling & Minting

Recyclers process the waste and earn digital RECO Points (RP) for every kilogram recycled through our 'GreenMint' module.

3

Industry Compliance

Manufacturers purchase RECO Points to offset their waste footprint, fulfilling their environmental responsibility via our 'PolluterPay' module.

4

Transaction & Revenue

All transactions occur on our transparent 'EcoLedger' platform, where recyclers sell points to manufacturers.

5

Community Incentives

Households and individuals earn rewards for segregating waste through our 'RewardFlow' program, closing the loop.

Our Core Benefits

Cleaner Environment

Dramatically reduce landfill waste and pollution by incentivizing recycling at a grassroots level.

Job Creation

Our system creates new opportunities in waste collection, sorting, and processing logistics.

Community Rewards

Users can earn valuable rewards, strengthening household and local business economies.

Industry Accountability

We provide transparent data that helps hold industries accountable for their production waste.

Our Roadmap

Phase 1: Pilot City

Launch and refine the complete RECO system within a single city to prove the model.

Phase 2: Regional Hubs

Expand to multiple cities and establish regional hubs for waste processing and management.

Phase 3: National Exchange

Develop a nationwide marketplace for RECO points, integrating with major industries.

Phase 4: International

Scale the RECO model globally, adapting it to different economies and waste streams.
